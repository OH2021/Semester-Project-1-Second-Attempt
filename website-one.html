WelcomeTo my top four websites

Alternative.me is a website that finds free alternatives for software with a paywall. E.g. you can search for Spotify (which is a software with a paywall, if you want to use the premium verison) in the search field, hit enter and you will get a window with information about Spotify and bellow you'll see a list with alternatives to Spotify which you can use. The alternatives isn't just software, but also possible to use in your browser. If you click on one of the alternatives you can visit their homepage by clicking the hyperlink in the window with the information for the respective alternative you clicked on.
